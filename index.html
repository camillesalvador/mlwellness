A spa experience with relaxing and wellness services.

Relaxation Massage

A gentle soothing, slow paced style that helps reduce muscle tension, improves circulation and recovery of soft tissue muscles. It also promotes reduction of stress hormones and anxiety.

Deep Tissue Massage

A deep, slow stroked to massage deeper muscles that helps reduce pain such as tight muscles and tension headaches that usually occurs with chronic pain.

Therapeutic Massage

A type of massage that involves more focused work on injured tissue that ease discomfort, increase range of motion and helps you heal faster from medical concerns.

Cupping Massage

A full body massage with a form of alternative treatment where a special cup creates suction which can move from place to place on the skin for a massage-like effect. It improves overall skin and muscles and helps increase blood.
